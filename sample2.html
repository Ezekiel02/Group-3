Basic Cutting tools in Photoshop LASSO TOOL, QUICK SELECTION TOOL AND PEN TOOL. These three kinds of cutting tools play an important role in Photoshop in which these three different tools also have different work that will apply in an area or portion of an image such as its shapes, designs as well as its background. LASSO TOOL Lasso tool operates on the active layer of an image, and is used by clicking and dragging to trace the edges of a selection. APPLICATION: First click the ribbon-shaped icon in the upper left panel menu to select the ribbon or press L to switch the keyboard shortcut to the tool. Once youâ€™ve selected your tool, you can click and drag the circular areas in the photo to quickly pick up and manipulate sections, make sure you close the selection or Photoshop will automatically do it for you wherever you stop to go.

QUICK SELECTION TOOL What is a Quick selection tool? The QUICK SELECTION TOOL is one of many selection tools in Photoshop. When you choose an area, Photoshop detects and selects the edges, so you don't have to trace them manually. This tool works great for photos with high contrast and well-defined edges. APPLICATION: To select the quick selection tool, click the icon of the paintbrush with the dotted line or hit the shift W shortcut to select it. To use the tool simply drag or paint over your image in the areas that you want to select.

PEN TOOL The Pen Tool in Photoshop creates paths and shapes which can be duplicated and manipulated to create complex selections, masks and objects. The pen tool is a path creator. You can create smooth paths that you can stroke with a brush or turn to a selection. This tool is effective for designing, selecting smooth surfaces, or layout. APPLICATION: Select the Pen tool using the shortcut P. To make a selection, click two points to create a line between them, and drag a point to create a curved line. Use Alt/opt-drag your lines to change them. Ctrl/right-click your path in the Paths tab on the right, and then choose Fill Path to create a shape from it.
